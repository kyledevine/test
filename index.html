State Temperature Change and Weather Events by Year



You've seen the uptick in severe weather events. You've seen the toll climate change takes. Now explore more and dive into the detail of how climate change and severe weather events have changed over time for the whole nation.

Use the timeline to change year, and hover over states to see the number of severe weather events.

Interact further by clicking the buttons below - you can filter severe weather events to look at specific types, hide weather events to focus just on climate variance, or transition the map to a bar-chart of states based on how many severe weather events have occurred in that year.


Disclaimer: We are taking a random sampling of the severe weather occurences per year due to the dataset size, and certain weather events were not properly documented over the years.
